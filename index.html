About

Born and raised in the suburbs outside of Detroit I grew up watching the Tigers in the summer and the Red Wings in the winter

After graduating undergrad I moved to the Washington DC metro area where I lived for 4 years working in various capacities while soaking up the culture and rich history the town had to offer.

In March 2008 my wife and I decided to relocate to Colorado in search of more outside activities! We love to hike, bike, camp and kayak so Colorado seemed like a natural fit for us.
